Welcome to the UMass Amherst campus! Before booking a meeting room, please take note of the following. If you are in an ECHO enabled room, this refers to Lecture Capture: Echo360; lecture capture technology allows you to record course content on your computers using the Echo360 Personal Capture software.

In addition, many classroom technology upgrades have been completed in the past year. These include “hybrid kits” (HK) now available in some classrooms and enhancements to classroom audiovisual equipment. In all technology-access labeled rooms, you will find an HDMI port and most likely projector access.

Finally, please note: no food or drinks allowed in academic classrooms.

For more information, please visit: https://www.umass.edu/it/technology-resources/teaching
